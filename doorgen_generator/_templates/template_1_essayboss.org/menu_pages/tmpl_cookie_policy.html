Cookies are essential for enhancing your browsing experience at EssayBoss.org. What is a cookie? It is a text record delivered from our servers to your computer. It is kept on your device and involves specific info that identifies you among additional users.

There are three types of cookies, but we only use two of these:

Session cookies are non permanent files retaining the information regarding your preferences at EssayBoss.org until your browser is start. Session cookies inform our servers if you are signed in and permit you to add what to your shopping basket.

Persistent cookies are retained for future visits to ensure that your browsing preferences could possibly be easily identified. You can delete these data files any moment (read more below).

We use cookies to:

  • Enable the core functionality of our website: disabling cookies won't let you place an order around
  • Verify if you are signed in. If you disable cookies, you will have to sign in each time the page reloads
  • Make the web chat communication possible
  • Store your preferences
  • Make use of Google Analytics and additional tracking systems to boost our website
  • Enable the sign-up process and commenting

Manage your cookies anytime
